Special responses for $$channel$$

When certain events happen, StilleBot can automatically react, to thank a person or celebrate an activity etc. Every event is triggered by somebody - the initiator of the special action - and many events have additional information available. For example, when someone cheers in your channel, StilleBot can thank the person with a message such as:

Thank you for the {bits} bits, $$$$!

MustardMine: This is a test cheer cheer100
StilleBot: Thank you for the 100 bits, MustardMine!
